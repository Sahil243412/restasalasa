About Restaurante Salas

Restaurante Salas has been serving locals and visitors since 1977. Situated near Plaza de Toros de la Real Maestranza, our restaurant combines timeless Andalusian décor and a welcoming ambience with high-quality Spanish and Mediterranean cuisine.

From traditional tapas and seafood to grilled meats and seasonal specials, we’re known for generous portions, friendly service, and a memorable dining experience.
